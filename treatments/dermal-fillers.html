What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable treatments that restore volume, smooth lines, and enhance facial contours. Made from hyaluronic acid (a substance naturally found in your skin), these treatments provide immediate, natural-looking results.

What Can Dermal Fillers Treat?

  • Lip enhancement and definition
  • Cheek volume and contouring
  • Nasolabial folds (smile lines)
  • Marionette lines
  • Jawline definition
  • Under-eye hollows
  • Chin enhancement
  • Temple restoration

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do dermal fillers last?

Results typically last 9-12 months, depending on the product used, treatment area, and individual metabolism. Lip fillers may last 6-9 months due to the increased movement in this area.

Is the treatment painful?

Most clients find the treatment very comfortable. We use topical numbing cream and many fillers contain lidocaine for additional comfort during injection.

What should I expect after treatment?

You may experience mild swelling, bruising, or redness at injection sites. This typically resolves within 2-7 days. Results improve as any swelling subsides.

When will I see results?

Results are immediately visible, though final results are best appreciated after 2 weeks once any swelling has completely resolved.

Can dermal fillers be reversed?

Yes, hyaluronic acid fillers can be dissolved using an enzyme called hyaluronidase if needed, making this a very safe treatment option.

Who is not suitable for dermal fillers?

Those who are pregnant, breastfeeding, have active skin infections, or severe allergies may not be suitable. We'll discuss your medical history during consultation.
