What Are Anti-Wrinkle Injections?

Anti-wrinkle injections use purified neurotoxins to temporarily relax specific facial muscles that cause dynamic wrinkles. This FDA-approved treatment is highly effective for smoothing existing lines and preventing new ones from forming.

What Can Anti-Wrinkle Injections Treat?

  • Forehead lines and horizontal wrinkles
  • Frown lines between eyebrows (11's)
  • Crow's feet around the eyes
  • Bunny lines on the nose
  • Gummy smile correction
  • Lip flip for subtle enhancement
  • Neck bands and turkey neck
  • Excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do anti-wrinkle injections last?

Results typically last 3-4 months. With regular treatments, the effects may last longer as muscles become trained to relax. Most clients return every 3-4 months for optimal results.

Does the treatment hurt?

Most clients describe the sensation as a small pinch. The needles used are ultra-fine, making the treatment very comfortable. No numbing is typically needed.

When will I see results?

You'll begin to notice improvements within 3-5 days, with full results visible after 14 days. The effects gradually develop as the muscles relax.

Will I look frozen or unnatural?

When administered by an expert, results look completely natural. We focus on softening lines while maintaining your natural expressions and facial movement.

Can I exercise after treatment?

Avoid vigorous exercise for 24 hours after treatment. Light activities are fine, but intense workouts should be postponed to ensure optimal results.

Are there any side effects?

Side effects are rare and mild. Some clients may experience slight redness or bruising at injection sites, which resolves within a few hours to days.
