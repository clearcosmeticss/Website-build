Package Questions

How much can I save with a package?

Package savings range from 20-25% compared to individual treatment pricing. The more comprehensive the package, the greater your savings.

Can I modify a package?

Absolutely! We can customize any package to better suit your needs and goals. Our team will work with you to create the perfect combination.

How are package treatments scheduled?

We coordinate your treatments for optimal timing and results. Some treatments can be done on the same day, while others are spaced for best outcomes.

What if I'm not happy with one treatment?

Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer complimentary consultations and adjustments to ensure you're happy with every aspect of your package.

Can I pause between treatments?

Yes, packages are flexible. You can schedule treatments over several months to fit your lifestyle and allow for optimal healing between procedures.

Do packages include follow-up care?

All packages include comprehensive aftercare, follow-up appointments, and support to ensure you achieve the best possible results.
