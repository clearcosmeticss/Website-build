What Are Polynucleotides?

Polynucleotides are naturally occurring biopolymers that play a crucial role in cellular regeneration and repair. When injected into the skin, they stimulate fibroblast activity, promote collagen synthesis, and improve skin hydration and elasticity at the molecular level.

  • Skin texture and smoothness
  • Hydration and moisture retention
  • Overall skin tone and radiance
  • Acne scarring and skin damage
  • Under-eye area and dark circles
  • Neck and décolletage aging

Frequently Asked Questions

How do polynucleotides work?

Polynucleotides work by activating fibroblasts and promoting cellular regeneration. They stimulate natural collagen and elastin production while improving skin hydration and repair mechanisms.

How many treatments do I need?

Most clients see excellent results with 2-3 treatments spaced 3-4 weeks apart. For optimal regeneration, we recommend a series of 3-4 treatments followed by maintenance sessions.

When will I see results?

Initial improvements appear within 2-3 weeks, with progressive enhancement over 3-6 months as collagen production increases. Results continue to improve with each treatment.

Is there any downtime?

Minimal downtime is required. You may experience slight swelling or redness for 24-48 hours. Most clients can return to normal activities immediately after treatment.

Are polynucleotides safe?

Yes, polynucleotides are naturally occurring substances that are biocompatible and have an excellent safety profile. They are gradually absorbed by the body without adverse effects.

Can polynucleotides be combined with other treatments?

Absolutely. Polynucleotides complement other aesthetic treatments beautifully and can be combined with dermal fillers, anti-wrinkle injections, or skin boosters for comprehensive rejuvenation.
