What Is Fat Dissolving?

Fat dissolving injections use a synthetic form of deoxycholic acid, a substance naturally produced by the body to break down fat. When injected into targeted areas, it permanently destroys fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by the body's lymphatic system.

What Areas Can Be Treated?

  • Double chin and submental fat
  • Jowls and jawline definition
  • Bra fat and underarm area
  • Love handles and flanks
  • Lower abdomen and muffin top
  • Inner and outer thighs
  • Knee fat and banana rolls
  • Upper arms and tricep area

Frequently Asked Questions

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients need 2-3 sessions spaced 6-8 weeks apart for optimal results. The number depends on the area size and amount of fat present.

Is the treatment painful?

Discomfort is moderate and manageable. We use topical numbing and ice to minimize pain. Most clients describe it as similar to having multiple injections.

What can I expect after treatment?

Expect significant swelling, bruising, and tenderness for 1-3 weeks. This is normal and indicates the treatment is working. Results become visible as swelling subsides.

Are the results permanent?

Yes, fat cells destroyed by the treatment are permanently eliminated. However, maintaining a stable weight through diet and exercise is important to preserve results.

Who is not suitable for this treatment?

Those who are pregnant, breastfeeding, have infections in the treatment area, or certain medical conditions may not be suitable. We'll assess during consultation.

How does it compare to liposuction?

Fat dissolving is non-surgical with no incisions or general anesthesia. While results are more gradual than liposuction, there's less risk and downtime involved.
