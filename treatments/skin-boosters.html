What Are Skin Boosters?

Skin boosters are injectable treatments that deliver hyaluronic acid directly into the skin's layers. Unlike traditional fillers that add volume, skin boosters improve overall skin quality by boosting hydration, elasticity, and radiance from within.

What Can Skin Boosters Improve?

  • Skin hydration and moisture retention
  • Fine lines and texture irregularities
  • Skin elasticity and firmness
  • Overall skin radiance and glow
  • Pore size and skin smoothness
  • Age-related skin dryness
  • Skin tone and evenness
  • Collagen production stimulation

Frequently Asked Questions

How do skin boosters differ from dermal fillers?

Skin boosters improve overall skin quality and hydration, while fillers add volume to specific areas. Skin boosters create a natural glow and better skin texture from within.

How many treatments do I need?

Most clients see excellent results with 2-3 initial treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart, followed by maintenance treatments every 6-9 months for optimal results.

Is there any downtime?

Minimal downtime is required. You may have tiny injection marks for a few hours and should avoid makeup for 6 hours. Most clients return to normal activities immediately.

When will I see results?

Initial improvements are visible within 1-2 weeks, with optimal results developing over 4-6 weeks as the hyaluronic acid integrates and stimulates collagen production.

Can skin boosters be combined with other treatments?

Yes, skin boosters complement other treatments beautifully. They can be combined with dermal fillers, anti-wrinkle injections, or professional skincare treatments.

Who is a good candidate for skin boosters?

Anyone wanting to improve skin quality, hydration, and radiance. They're particularly beneficial for those with dehydrated skin, fine lines, or wanting to prevent aging.
